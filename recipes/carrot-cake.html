Carrot Cake



Description

This is the best carrot cake recipe I have ever made! It's so moist and flavorful and makes a large quantity of cake.
Since I started making it, I have been hounded to make this cake time and time again. Frost with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple, drained
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
